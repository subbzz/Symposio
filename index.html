Features of Symposio

  • Recipe Management: Add and organise your recipes effortlessly.
  • Apple ID Sign-In: Secure login using your Apple ID.
  • Dark Mode: Enjoy a sleek, comfortable interface at any time.
  • Face ID: Quickly access your app with Face ID authentication.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I sign in?

Symposio uses Apple ID for secure and seamless sign-in. Tap the "Sign In with Apple" button on the login screen to get started.

2. Can I switch between Metric and US units?

Yes, you can switch between Metric and US units in the app's settings to suit your preference.

3. How do I group my recipes by cuisine?

When adding a recipe, select a cuisine type from the dropdown menu. This will automatically group it under the selected cuisine.

4. Can I add links to external resources?

Yes, you can add URLs from YouTube, Instagram, or other websites to enrich your recipes with helpful videos or images.

5. How do I list ingredients like fruits and vegetables?

When creating a recipe, use the ingredient input fields to add items such as fruits and vegetables. You can also categorise them for better organisation.
